Securing Democracy Against Foreign Interference

SAUFEX (Secure Automated Unified Framework for Exchange) is an EU-funded collaborative initiative designed to combat foreign information manipulation and interference. By providing a secure and automated platform for data exchange, SAUFEX facilitates unified efforts among member states and partners to detect, analyse, and counteract the tactics used by foreign entities to undermine democratic processes. 

Our mission is to safeguard democratic processes through innovative technology and global collaboration.

  • Detect and Analyze Disinformation
  • Enhance Democratic Resilience
  • Foster International Collaboration
  • Automate Governance Decisionmaking
  • Train Officials in the EU

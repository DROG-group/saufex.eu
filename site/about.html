About SAUFEX

Secure Automated Unified Framework for Exchange - An EU-funded initiative protecting democracy from foreign interference.

Our Mission

SAUFEX (Secure Automated Unified Framework for Exchange) is an EU-funded collaborative initiative designed to combat foreign information manipulation and interference (FIMI) through innovative technology and global collaboration.

We protect authentic expression from fabricated, disingenuous, or coordinated interference by developing tools, frameworks, and training programs that enhance democratic resilience across Europe and beyond.

Our Objectives

Detect & Analyze

Develop automated systems to detect and analyze disinformation campaigns in real-time.

Build Resilience

Establish resilience councils and training programs for officials and practitioners.

Foster Collaboration

Create networks for international cooperation on information integrity issues.

Share Knowledge

Publish research, develop educational resources, and disseminate best practices.
